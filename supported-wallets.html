Understanding USDT Networks

USDT runs on multiple blockchain networks. Choose the right one for your needs based on speed, cost, and compatibility.

Lowest Fees
TRC-20 (Tron)
Best for: Daily transactions
Ultra-low fees (~$1) and fast confirmation times. Most popular choice for P2P trading.
~$1 Fee 3-5 mins
ERC-20 (Ethereum)
Best for: DeFi and large amounts
Original USDT network with highest liquidity. Higher fees but maximum compatibility.
$5-50 Fee 2-15 mins
BEP-20 (BSC)
Best for: Trading and DeFi
Binance Smart Chain offers low fees and fast transactions. Great middle ground option.
~$0.50 Fee 1-3 mins
Polygon
Best for: Micro-transactions
Ethereum Layer 2 with extremely low fees. Perfect for frequent small transactions.
~$0.01 Fee 1-2 mins
