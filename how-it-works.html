How MalluCrypto Works

Experience secure, transparent, and hassle-free USDT trading with our proven 5-step process. Every transaction is protected by our advanced escrow system.

Our Trading Process

Follow these simple steps to trade USDT safely and securely with verified peers

1
Contact & Requirements
Reach out to us via WhatsApp with your trading requirements. Specify the amount of USDT you want to buy or sell, your preferred payment method, and any specific needs.
2
Peer Matching
Our team matches you with verified, trusted peers from our network. We ensure all parties are KYC verified and have a proven track record of successful transactions.
3
Escrow Protection
USDT is securely held in our escrow system until both parties confirm the transaction. This ensures complete protection for both buyers and sellers throughout the process.
4
Payment Verification
Once payment is made via your chosen method (bank transfer, UPI, cash), we verify the transaction with both parties before proceeding to the final step.
5
Secure Release
After payment confirmation, USDT is released from escrow to the buyer's wallet. Transaction complete! Both parties receive confirmation and transaction records.
