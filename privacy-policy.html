🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024
⸻
1. Introduction
⸻
MalluCrypto is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our peer-to-peer USDT trading platform.
By using MalluCrypto, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.
2. Information We Collect
⸻
Personal Information:
  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Government-issued ID documents for KYC verification
  • Bank account details and payment information
  • Cryptocurrency wallet addresses
  • Profile photos and verification selfies
Transaction Information:
  • Trading history and transaction records
  • Payment confirmations and receipts
  • Communication logs with trading partners
  • Dispute resolution records
Technical Information:
  • IP address, device information, browser type
  • Usage patterns and platform interactions
  • Security logs and access records
3. How We Use Your Information
⸻
Platform Operations:
  • Verify your identity and comply with KYC/AML regulations
  • Facilitate secure P2P USDT transactions
  • Manage escrow services and fund releases
  • Match you with verified trading partners
  • Process payments and maintain transaction records
Security & Fraud Prevention:
  • Monitor for suspicious activities and fraud
  • Investigate and resolve disputes
  • Maintain platform security and integrity
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
Customer Support:
  • Provide technical support and assistance
  • Respond to inquiries and feedback
  • Improve our services based on user feedback
4. Information Sharing & Disclosure
⸻
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
With Trading Partners:
Limited information (name, rating, transaction history) may be shared to facilitate trades.
Legal Requirements:
When required by law, court order, or regulatory authorities.
Service Providers:
Trusted third-party services for KYC verification, payment processing, and technical support.
Business Transfers:
In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets.
Safety & Security:
To protect the rights, property, or safety of MalluCrypto, users, or the public.
5. Data Security
⸻
We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:
  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Advanced firewall protection and intrusion detection
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Employee background checks and confidentiality agreements
  • Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures
However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
6. Data Retention
⸻
We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:
  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security
Retention Periods:
  • Account information: 7 years after account closure
  • Transaction records: 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • KYC documents: 5 years after verification
  • Communication logs: 3 years for support purposes
7. Your Privacy Rights
⸻
You have the following rights regarding your personal information:
  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing
To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mallucrypto.com
8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies
⸻
Essential Cookies:
We use minimal cookies necessary for platform functionality, security, and user authentication.
Analytics:
We may use privacy-focused analytics to improve our services without tracking personal information.
No Third-Party Tracking:
We do not use advertising cookies or allow third-party tracking on our platform.
9. International Data Transfers
⸻
Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:
  • Standard contractual clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy decisions for countries with equivalent protection
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct
10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.
